FINEQ便携式蓝牙音箱Alcor

产品图库

设计说明

In our experience many people enjoy bringing a portable speaker with them when engaging outdoor activities, so we wanted to design a portable speaker specifically for the outdoor enthusiasts. From the acoustics to the design details, Alcor is a thoughtfully crafted way to stream music from your phone to enjoy, share and ‘decorate’ your journey with music. It’s a great companion speaker while you are walking, hiking, working out, tailgating, relaxing on the patio or entertaining guests in your backyard.

With a pair of custom-made full-range drivers, a tweeter, and a pair of carefully tuned passive raditors, Alcor is acoustically balanced to complement anyone’s musical taste. Alcor is ready to bring anywhere on-the-go with its easy-to-carry handle and built-in rechargeable Lithium-ion battery for up to seven hours of AC-free listening. It also includes a Type-C USB charging port so users can charge their Alcor with any ubiquitous Type-C cables, at home, in office, or in any hotel room.

With today’s unlimited access to streaming music and video services, Alcor allows users to bring their music, TV shows and movies to life and share their listening experience with friends and family. Alcor continues to serve FINEQ’s commitment to delivering a versatile, wireless audio entertainment experience, and is packed with features including:

  • 2.1Ch acoustically balanced sound

  • Built-in rechargeable battery offers up to five hours of AC-free listening

  • Robust carrying handle, a convenient design for on-the-go use applications

  • Universal Type-C USB charging port to charge with any ubiquitous Type-C cables anywhere

  • Line-in available to plug and play any device with a standard headphone jack

  • Music playback for audio files stored in a USB flash drive

  • FM radio

  • Water resistance level IPX5, splash-proof and rainproof, perfectly designed for outdoor scenarios such as mounted on a bike, in a boat, on the beach, at the poolside, and more

  • TWS - True Wireless Stereo, allows for two Alcor systems to be paired together to play as one speaker system with left and right audio split between each for true stereo separation and powerful sound.

  • Three different modes of RGB Lighting

功能特色

  • 2.1通道大音量便携式蓝牙音箱

  • IPX5防水级别，防水溅，防雨淋

  • 支持蓝牙无线，AUX IN，U盘，FM收音机

  • 支持TWS(True Wireless System) - 真无线蓝牙立体声

  • 支持RGB彩灯 - 三种亮炫效果

  • 内置锂电池 3.7V 3000mAh - 正常音量欣赏音乐持续4-5小时无压力

  • 固定提手 + 不锈钢背带挂扣
